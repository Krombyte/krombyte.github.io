About the 2024 Dodge Challenger

The Dodge Challenger brings back the 1970s attitude with 21st-century performance. Known for its wide stance and pure muscle personality, it’s one of the last true American coupes.

Trims & Horsepower Options

  • SXT — 303 HP (V6)
  • R/T — 375 HP (5.7L V8)
  • Scat Pack — 485 HP (6.4L V8)
  • Hellcat — 717 HP (Supercharged V8)
  • Super Stock — 807 HP (Drag-tuned version)
